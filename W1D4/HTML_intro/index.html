Ninja roll info

Ingredients

  • Crab
  • Eel
  • Volcano sauce
  • Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Cream cheese

Instructions

  1. go fishing
  2. harvest rice
  3. cook rice
  4. cover one side of sea weed with rice
  5. put eel, crab, volcano sauce, and cream cheese on seaweed rice
  6. using a bamboo mat, roll the ninja roll
  7. enjoy ;)
